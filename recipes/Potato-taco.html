Potato Taco

Description

Describing recipe.

More description of the recipe.

Ingredients

  • One ripe Avocado
  • Two slices of sourdough bread
  • Three-four cherry/angel tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper and blue cheese.

Steps

  1. Scoop avocado out of its 'skin' and mash it with a fork.
  2. Put your bread in a pan on a medium heat and sprinkle it with oil.
  3. Crisp it. Slice tomatoes.
  4. Spread avocado pasta on bread. Add cheese on top.
